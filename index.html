Cyclone2026: ZX Rescue

Collect 5 crates, rescue optional refugees, return to base and land before fuel/time run out.

How to Play

Retrieve all 5 crates from islands, then land at base helipad to win the round. Rescue refugees for bonus score.

Cyclone proximity causes wind drift. Near center is fatal. Planes can crash into you over water routes.
